Prometheus

A 2D Platformer

Check Out This Project

In this game you play as Prometheus, you just stole fire from the gods and are now trying to get to earth to give it to the humans. But the gods found out and sent some Aliens after you. So get ready for a fight!

This game is a 2D Platformer with a pixel art aesthetic. The objective of the game is to reach a portal at the end of the level. Until then, the player will find several types of enemies that will try to kill him (something that forces the player to restart a certain part of the level, depending on the spawn location) and several types of platforms that intend to reproduce the same effect.

The player has five hearts (life), each one equivalent to the number of hits it can take from enemies. The fall of the map puts life to zero.
