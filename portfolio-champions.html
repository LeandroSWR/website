Champions of Tomorrow

A First-Person Online Arena Shooter

Check out this project:

A frenetic online multiplayer FPS set in a futuristic world. Choose your champion, select your abilities and fight your foes in quick 4v4 matches.

In our game, players get to adapt their characters according to their own playstyles. Before a game starts, the player can choose 2 abilities (also known as Blessings) per character, from a total of 4, each one completely changing the way characters are played.

• Abilities: Interchangeable and completely unique.

• Progression: Unlocking abilities via tokens, obtainable after leveling up.

• Characters: Diverse with distinguished gameplay.
